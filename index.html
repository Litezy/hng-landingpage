Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Still have questions that aren't covered? visit our help desk today and get the answers you need.

Contact our Help Desk

What is the Internship all about?

It is virtual and remote internship that enables you learn and improve your skills by working on real-world projects. It is for people learning to code/design/manage and want to accelerate their knowledge.

What benefits can I get from the internship?

You will rapidly improve your technical skills. You will meet other smart people. You will build a great network that will serve you for life..

When will the Internship begin??

Program starts on the 8th of September, 2023, follow on on twitter @hnginternship, or on Telegram: https://t.me/hnginternship.

How long does the internship last?

About 7 weeks.

What tracks are available??

We offer Backend, Frontend, DevOps, Mobile, UI/UX, Sales/Marketing and Management tracks.

Will I be employed by Zuri/HNG upon completion of the internship?

You will be added to our network of talent, and recommended to potential employers. We have had good success with people getting employed, but we are not promising anything.

Will mentors be available for physical mentorship?

Meetups will be held where mentors around your region will be available to answer to any question.

Do I get to have a personal mentor?

Mentors can be contacted privately for guidance during the internship.

How often do we build new projects?

Every week new projects are assigned to interns as tasks to complete and present to be promoted to the next stage.

I cannot apply because it keeps saying record already exists, why?

This means you have already applied for this program, and your details are registered with us.
